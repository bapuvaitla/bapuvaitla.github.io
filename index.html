1. The ancient history of numbers

We don't often stop to think about what numbers are. It helps to consider where numbers came from, and to do that it's useful to take an evolutionary look at the purpose of numbers. The earliest linguistic evidence of number...But writing is the most concrete evidence we have of the existence of a number system, and so the story begins, as it often does, with the Sumerians, the first known civilization to have a writing system.
